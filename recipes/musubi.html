Musubi

Description

A musubi is hawaiian delicacy. It is very easy to make

Ingredients

  • 1 can of spam
  • Rice
  • Soy Sauce
  • Furikake
  • Nori

Steps

  1. Cook spam to crisp in pan
  2. Cook rice
  3. Place cooked rice on a square piece of nori
  4. Add shoyu and furikake for flavor
  5. Add spam
  6. Roll musubi up
  7. Serve
