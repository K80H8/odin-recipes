Beef Stew

Description

Beef Stew is like soul food. It's delicious, it's hearty and packed with protein

Ingredients

  • 1lb of chuck meat
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Celery
  • Carrots
  • Onions
  • 1 Beef Stew Sauce Packet

Steps

  1. Throw the chuck meat in a pot, and brown the beef stew meat
  2. Add 1 cup of water to pot
  3. Mix in packet of sauce
  4. Boil for 2 hours on low
  5. Add vegetables
  6. Let it simmer for 30 minutes
  7. Let it sit for 20 minutes, and then serve
