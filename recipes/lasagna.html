Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a staple in our household. It is a delicious layered pasta

Ingredients

  • 1lb of hamburger
  • Pasta Sauce
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Spinach
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Parmesan

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Boil lasagna noodles
  3. Brown hamburger
  4. Layer noodles, sauce, hamburger, ricotta, and spinach
  5. Repeat layers
  6. Top with parmesan
  7. Bake for 30 minutes
